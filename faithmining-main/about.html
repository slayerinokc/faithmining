What is Faith Mining?

Faith Mining is a Bitcoin mining education site. Every article here is written from hands-on experience operating actual mining hardware — not from aggregating news or repeating what other sites say.

The site covers the practical side of Bitcoin mining: how to calculate profitability, how to evaluate equipment, what network difficulty actually means for your bottom line, and when the math works in your favor versus when it doesn't. The goal is to give you the information you need to make real decisions — with real numbers, not optimistic projections.

Who writes here?

My name is Chris Johnson. I'm an IT specialist with a background in systems and infrastructure, and I started Bitcoin mining as a way to build income outside of a traditional job. I run Faith Mining LLC as an umbrella for my mining activities and the tools and content I create around them.

I started this site because most Bitcoin mining content online falls into one of two categories: either it's wildly optimistic marketing from hardware sellers, or it's surface-level explanations that don't help you actually run an operation. I wanted to write the guides I wished existed when I started — honest, specific, and based on what the numbers actually show.

What you won't find here

No get-rich-quick promises. No "mining is always profitable" claims. No hiding the numbers when they're bad. Bitcoin mining is a capital-intensive activity with real costs and real risk. This site will tell you that plainly, because you deserve to know what you're getting into before you spend money on equipment or power.

Nothing on this site is financial advice. I'm not a financial advisor, and you should treat everything here as educational information to inform your own research and decisions.
