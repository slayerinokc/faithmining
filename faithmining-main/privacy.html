Privacy Policy

Faith Mining LLC  ·  Last updated: April 29, 2026  ·  Applies to faithmining.net and tools.faithmining.net

Overview

Faith Mining LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates faithmining.net and tools.faithmining.net. We are committed to being transparent about how our sites work and what data, if any, is collected when you use them.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our calculator tools are entirely client-side. We do not:

  • Require you to create an account or log in
  • Ask for your name, email address, or any personal information
  • Store any of the values you enter into our calculators
  • Sell or share personal data with any third party

Third-Party APIs

Our Bitcoin miner tools fetch live market data from the following public APIs to display current prices and network difficulty:

  • CoinGecko — real-time BTC price (api.coingecko.com)
  • Coinbase — BTC price fallback (api.coinbase.com)
  • Blockchain.info — Bitcoin network difficulty (blockchain.info)

These requests are made directly from your browser. We do not proxy or log them. Each provider has its own privacy policy.

Cookies and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our sites. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our websites or other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our sites, visit How Google uses data from sites that use its services.

If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, Faith Mining LLC may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Affiliate links are clearly labeled with a disclosure note near each set of links.

Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand overall traffic patterns (page views, referral sources). We do not use analytics to build profiles of individual users.

Our sites may link to external websites (GitHub, Amazon, hardware retailers, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.
