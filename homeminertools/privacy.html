Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Home Miner Tools (homeminertools.com) is operated by Faith Mining LLC. This privacy policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. We are committed to handling your data responsibly.

Information we collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us via email. When you use the calculators on this site, all calculations are performed locally in your browser — no input data is sent to our servers.

We use third-party services that may collect information automatically:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements and may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your interests. Google's advertising cookies may track your browsing behavior across websites.
  • Google Analytics (if enabled): Collects anonymized usage data including pages visited, session duration, and general geographic location. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Google Fonts: Loads fonts from Google's servers, which may log your IP address as part of that request.

Cookies

This site uses cookies primarily through Google AdSense for advertising purposes. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out.

Your browser settings also allow you to control or delete cookies at any time.

Third-party links and affiliate disclosures

This site contains affiliate links to third-party retailers. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. All affiliate relationships are disclosed clearly on the relevant pages.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites we link to. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before making any purchase.

Email contact

If you contact us at info@homeminertools.com, we will use your email address only to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, trade, or share email addresses with any third parties.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be reflected by the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
